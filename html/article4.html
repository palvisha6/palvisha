My App:
WikiRacer

Written By Palvisha Shoaib

WikiRacer is a simple and fun iOS game based on the popular Wiki game. In this game, the player
is given a beginning and target Wiki article. They start off on the beginning article and try to
navigate to the target one in the fastest time or least amount of referential links possible. Our team
will further gamify this app by supporting features like both single and multiplayer mode as well as an
in-app racer character whom you can customize and design using points won in games.

For single player mode, the game can be played by an individual in a time trial environment where they can
race against their own previous records and try to make it on a global leaderboard for specific topic pairings.
The player would have the option to play a random game or choose their own Wiki article topics.

However, WikiRacerâ€™s main attraction is multiplayer mode! Multiplayer offers two different selections:
joining a random game or creating a game. Joining a random game will pair the user with other players and
start a game. On the other hand, creating a game allows the user to have an invite code for friends to join
and the host of the game has the option to choose a random game or to pre-select the topics.

If having a super fun game to play is not enough, WikiRacer has in-game points you can use to customize your
personal racer character. The game offers a store with different accessories and backgrounds that are available
to redeem for your character. There is also a character inventory feature, where you can pick and choose from
the different accessories and backgrounds you have for your character to show off to others.
